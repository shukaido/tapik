Tapik Interactive Demo Guide

Follow this guide step-by-step to experience the full order flow across Consumer and Merchant apps in real time.

Before You Start

⚠ IMPORTANT: Two-Tab Setup Required

Tapik's Consumer and Merchant apps communicate in real time via BroadcastChannel API. To see the full experience — orders appearing on the merchant side, status changes reflecting back on the consumer side — you must open both apps in the same browser.

1
Open Consumer App

Open Consumer App in a new tab. This is your primary view — the customer's mobile experience.

2
Open Merchant App (side by side)

Open Merchant App in another tab. Then arrange both tabs side by side (Windows: Win+← / Win+→, Mac: drag to screen edge). The Merchant app simulates a Lenovo Tab M7 tablet in landscape mode — the device merchants would actually use.

3
Verify Connection

In the Merchant app, check the header area — you should see a green indicator showing "Consumer Online." This confirms BroadcastChannel is working and both apps can communicate.

💡 What You'll Experience

When you place an order in the Consumer app, it appears instantly in the Merchant app's order queue. When the merchant accepts, prepares, and marks ready — the Consumer app updates in real time. No page refresh needed. This is the core of Tapik: a direct line between customer and kitchen, no middleman.

Consumer App — Step-by-Step Walkthrough

The Consumer app is built as a mobile-first progressive web app inside a phone-frame simulation. All core flows — from login to order pickup — are fully functional with Firebase backend.

Step 1: Login with Google

1
Tap "Start with Google"

The app uses Firebase Authentication with Google Sign-In. Tap the button, select your Google account, and you're in. Your profile (name, photo) is pulled from your Google account automatically.

LIVE Firebase Auth — real authentication, your account is created in Firestore.

Step 2: Explore the Home Screen

2
Browse the Home Tab

After login, you land on the Home tab. Here you'll see:

Nearby Stores — A list of F&B merchants. Each card shows the store name, cuisine type, distance, rating, and live queue status (e.g., "3 ahead · ~9 min"). Queue numbers are calculated in real time from actual orders in Firestore.

"Right Now" Cards — A horizontal carousel showing live deals: queue alerts, spot discounts (2× points during off-peak), and hot deals (clearance items with 3× points).

Friend Feed — Shows where friends are eating. MOCK DATA

Step 3: Enter a Store & Browse Menu

3
Tap the "Feeka Coffee Roasters" store card

⚠ You must select Feeka. The Merchant app is connected to Feeka Coffee Roasters, so you need to order from this store to experience the real-time sync — order received → accepted → prepared → pickup — on the merchant side.

The store detail view opens, showing:

Store Info — Operating status (Open/Closed), rating, distance, current queue. A cover photo area with Favorite and Share buttons.

Menu Items — Each item shows name, description, price (RM), and an Add (+) button. Items are categorized but displayed in a single scrollable list.

"Room" Button — Opens the Room creation flow (covered in Step 8).

👉 Try it: Add 2–3 items to your cart. You'll see a floating cart bar appear at the bottom showing item count and total.

Step 4: Cart & Checkout

4
Tap the cart bar at the bottom

The checkout screen shows your order summary:

Item List — Each item with quantity controls (−/+) and line total.

Subtotal — Sum of all items at menu price.

Service Fee (5%) — This is the core of Tapik's model. The 5% fee is charged to the consumer, and 100% of it is returned as loyalty points (TP).

Points to Earn — Dynamically calculated based on your role: Solo order = 5% back, Room Joiner = 6%, Room Creator = 8% (up to 10% in Hotspot mode).

Payment Method — Displays Touch 'n Go eWallet. UI ONLY No actual payment processing.

👉 Try it: Tap "Pay & Pre-order" to place your order.

Step 5: Order Tracking (Pre-paid State)

5
Your order is now placed

After payment, you enter the Order tab automatically. Your order appears as a "Pre-paid" card with two options:

"Order Now" — Sends the order to the merchant immediately. The order status changes to ORDERED and appears in the Merchant app's queue.

"Save for Later" — Keeps the prepaid order for up to 24 hours. You can submit it when you're ready (e.g., pre-pay at 11 AM, send to kitchen at 12 PM).

👉 Try it: Tap "Order Now" and immediately look at the Merchant app tab.

REAL-TIME SYNC The order should appear in the Merchant app's "New" column within 1–2 seconds.

Step 6: Switch to Merchant App (Process the Order)

🔄 Now switch to the Merchant App tab

This is where the magic happens. You'll process the order from the merchant's perspective, and watch the Consumer app update in real time.

6a
Accept the Order (Merchant App)

In the Merchant app's Orders tab, you'll see the new order in the "New" column. Tap "Accept". The order moves to the "Preparing" column with a preparation checklist.

👉 Look at the Consumer app: The order status changes from "ORDERED" to "PREPARING" with an animated transition.

6b
Mark Ready (Merchant App)

When "preparation is complete," tap "Ready" in the Merchant app. The order moves to the "Awaiting Pickup" column.

👉 Look at the Consumer app: Status changes to "READY" — a QR code grid appears for pickup verification.

Step 7: Pickup & Points Earned

7
Complete Pickup (Merchant App)

In the Merchant app, tap "QR Pickup" (simulates scanning the customer's QR code). The order status changes to PICKED_UP.

👉 Look at the Consumer app:

• The order shows "PICKED UP ✓" with a celebration animation.

• Points (TP) are automatically credited to your account — check the Rewards tab to see your updated balance.

• A review prompt appears — you can leave a star rating and optional comment.

LIVE Points are written to Firestore in real time. The entire flow — order to pickup to points — is fully functional.

✅ You just completed the full Tapik order cycle

Consumer pre-pays → Merchant receives → Prepares → Ready → Pickup → Points earned. All synced in real time across two browser tabs, backed by Firebase Firestore. No page refreshes, no polling — pure real-time architecture.

Step 8: Room — Group Ordering

8
Create or Join a Room

Go to the Room tab in the Consumer app. Rooms are Tapik's social ordering feature — multiple people order together from the same store and everyone earns boosted points.

Create Room:

• Choose a store, set room type (Public or Private with password), set member limit (2–8).

• Your room appears in the live room list. Other users can see and join Public rooms.

• As Creator, you control when ordering begins ("Activate Ordering") and can destroy the room.

Room State Machine:

Waiting → Ready (min members met) → Ordering → All Paid → Closing → Deleted

Points Boost: Creator earns 8% (vs. solo 5%), Joiners earn 6%. In Hotspot mode, Creator can earn up to 10%.

LIVE Full Firestore CRUD — rooms are created, joined, and destroyed in real time. Auto-expires after 30 min if minimum members aren't met.

Step 9: Rewards & Points

9
Check Your Points

Go to the Rewards tab to see:

Total TP Balance — Your accumulated loyalty points. Shows a progress bar toward the TP Cap (100,000 TP = RM 1,000 maximum).

Level System — L1 Newbie → L2 Regular (500+ TP) → L3 Local (5,000+ TP). Level determines access to future features.

By-Store Breakdown — Shows how many points you've earned at each store. LIVE

Quests — Achievement-style missions (visit 3 stores, try a new cafe, join a room). MOCK DATA

Rewards Shop — Catalog of redeemable rewards (free coffee, coupons, kaya toast). MOCK DATA

Step 10: Language Toggle & Settings

10
Switch Language

Go to the Me tab and tap the language toggle (EN/KO). The entire app switches between English and Korean instantly — 500+ translation keys covering all UI text.

REAL-TIME SYNC Language change propagates to the Merchant and Admin apps via BroadcastChannel — all three apps switch language simultaneously.

Admin App — Overview

📋 A Note on Admin App

The Admin app serves as Tapik's internal operations dashboard. It is primarily built with mock/placeholder data to demonstrate the intended analytics and management capabilities. The live order feed and reward mode settings are the only fully functional features connected to Firebase. All other metrics, charts, and tables show representative sample data.

The Admin app uses a dark theme dashboard layout designed for the Tapik operations team.

Technical Summary

Component Technology Details
Architecture Single-file HTML + React 18 + Babel No build step. Each app is one .html file. Open in browser = running app.
Backend Firebase Firestore (serverless) Real-time NoSQL database. All order data, user profiles, room states, and reward settings live here.
Authentication Firebase Auth (Google Sign-In) One-tap login. User profile synced to Firestore on first sign-in.
Cross-App Sync BroadcastChannel API Browser-native. Consumer ↔ Merchant ↔ Admin communicate order events and language changes without server round-trips.
Localization Custom i18n (601 keys) English + Korean. Language persists in localStorage, syncs across all apps.
Code Volume 5,168 lines total Consumer: 2,757 / Merchant: 1,013 / Admin: 1,398
